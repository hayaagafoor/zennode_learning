WordPress

What is WordPress?

WordPress is a free and open-source content management system (CMS) that allows users to create and manage websites without deep coding knowledge. It is used for blogs, business websites, eCommerce stores, and portfolios.

What is WordPress used for?

  • Blogs and personal websites
  • Business and corporate sites
  • Online stores (WooCommerce)
  • News portals and forums

Websites Powered by WordPress

Over 43% of all websites on the internet are powered by WordPress. This includes major brands like BBC, Sony Music, and TechCrunch.
